To Infinity and Beyond - CyberSec with Alex Behar and ECL

Launched in 1998 by Alex Behar and co, ECL is a cybersecurity research organization focusing on community. What we all share is a love for breaking things with an eye on rebuilding something better. Our research has been featured in leading journals, newspapers, popular media, and various commercial products.

Publications Who is Alex?

Exploring Cybersecurity, one IoC at a Time

Like exploring space, you never know what you’ll find next in the security research industry. We’ve been to some fantastic places, met amazing people, and worked with cutting-edge technologies. Our mission is to create an encyclopedia of all our knowledge under the non-for-profit ECL banner - freely accessible for all.

While most of the space is still a mystery, we’ve worked hard to uncover all we can in the cybersecurity landscape. There’s no corner of the cybersecurity space we haven’t explored, from embedded, IoT, telemetry, and guidance systems to GPS and satellite communications.

No Bug is Too Small

As cybersecurity enthusiasts, we’ve developed an uncanny knack for spotting bugs and vulnerabilities in the unlikeliest places. No flaw can escape our magnifying glass, and our unflinching curiosity drives us to dig as deep as we need to. Bugs beware!

Counting Down The Inevitable Era of Industrial Automation

IoT and the Fourth Industrial Revolution are set to shake up the cybersecurity industry. Small devices already surround us, with billions more joining the swarm each year. We’re committed to discovering how this trend will affect the cybersecurity industry and our society.
